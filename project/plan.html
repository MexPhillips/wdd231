Uganda Arsenal Fan Community

Project Site Plan

Site Name

Uganda Arsenal Fan Community

This name reflects the purpose of uniting Arsenal supporters in Uganda under one hub.

Site Purpose

The site provides a platform for Arsenal fans in Uganda to connect, share match updates, attend events, and access resources like live scores and team news.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find details about the next Arsenal match screening in Jinja?
  • How do I join the Uganda Arsenal fan club?

Color Scheme

Main Colors: Arsenal Red (#EF0107), White (#FFFFFF), Dark Gray (#333333)

Red for headings and accents, white for body text, gray for backgrounds and secondary elements.

Typography

Fonts: Montserrat (headings), Roboto (body text)

Wireframes

Home Page Layout:

  • Mobile: Logo at top, navigation menu, hero banner, content blocks stacked vertically.
  • Desktop: Logo and nav bar at top, hero banner across, three-column content layout.
